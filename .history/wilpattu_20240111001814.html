Discover Yala

Yala (යාල) National Park

Yala National Park, situated in the southeastern part of Sri Lanka, is renowned for its diverse wildlife and stunning natural landscapes. Spanning over 979 square kilometers, the park is home to a variety of ecosystems, including scrub jungles, grasslands, and coastal lagoons.

About The Wildlife

Sri Lanka, a gem in the Indian Ocean, captivates visitors with its rich culture, stunning beaches, and diverse wildlife. Land attractions include elephants, monkeys, leopards, and sloth bears, while offshore, whales and dolphins offer a mesmerizing spectacle.
